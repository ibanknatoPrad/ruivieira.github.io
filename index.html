Welcome

Welcome to my site, ruivieira.dev.

Formerly, this site used to be a blog. Currently, it is adhering to the principles of a Digital Garden and following the guidelines of Brutalist Web Design. It is intended to be a collection of notes, a learning journal and a reference – all growing organically. Parts of this site and other additional content (including a gemlog) are available at ruivieira.srht.site (using Gemini).

Technical details about this site are available on the site details page.
